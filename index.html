Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you make the right choice

What types of plywood do you offer?

We offer Commercial Plywood, BWR (Boiling Water Resistant), BWP (Boiling Water Proof), Marine Plywood, Pre-laminated Plywood, and Calibrated Plywood. All available in various thicknesses from 4mm to 25mm.

What is the difference between BWR and BWP plywood?

BWR (Boiling Water Resistant) plywood can withstand moisture but not prolonged water exposure. BWP (Boiling Water Proof) is fully waterproof and ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and outdoor use. BWP is more expensive but lasts longer in wet conditions.

Which laminate finish is best for kitchens?

For kitchens, we recommend matte or textured finish laminates as they hide fingerprints and scratches better. High-gloss laminates look stunning but require more maintenance. Anti-fingerprint laminates are also available for a clean look with easy upkeep.

Do you offer delivery? What's the delivery time?

Yes! We offer same-day delivery within Ahmedabad city for in-stock items. Express delivery within 2 hours is available for urgent requirements. For bulk orders or special items, delivery is typically 1-3 days.

What brands of Fevicol and adhesives do you stock?

We stock the complete Fevicol range including Fevicol SH, Marine, Heatproof, SpeedX, SR998, and Hi-Per. We also carry Araldite, Fevibond, and other specialty adhesives for different applications.

How much plywood do I need for a standard kitchen?

A standard modular kitchen (10x10 ft) typically requires 18-25 sheets of plywood (8x4 ft). This varies based on design complexity, number of cabinets, and accessories. Use our AI Material Calculator for a more accurate estimate!

Do you provide CNC cutting services?

Yes, we offer precision CNC cutting services for custom jali work, designer patterns, 3D carvings, and MDF routing. Bring your design or choose from our 200+ ready patterns. Turnaround time is 2-5 days depending on complexity.

What is the minimum order quantity?

No minimum order! Whether you need a single sheet or a full truckload, we serve all order sizes with the same care. Even small quantities get instant delivery.
